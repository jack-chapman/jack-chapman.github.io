Hello, I'm Jack

I like making cool stuff with web technologies. I'm not a pro, but I'm getting there.

Who am I?

I am a music technology graduate keen to learn new skills. I spent a lot of time making strange music and learning how to approach problems with an alternative (hopefully creative) point of view. I've more recently spent my time learning how to be more logical through learning code. It has captivated me.

It started with Codecademy's Python course, which was brilliant. I have, however, forgotten almost all of it. After that I decided to delve into game development and tried my had at becoming a Unity developer with C#. Rather quickly I realised that I was far out of my depth (at the time). Feeling a bit dejected and disappointed, I took a hiatus from code.

Eventually I decided to restart with JavaScript, followed by HTML and CSS. The ability to instantly see your code working and running in the browser grabbed me.
